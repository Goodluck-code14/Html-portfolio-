The Best Movies According To Dev Goodluck

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Marty supreme

Marty Supreme thrives as an exercise in kinetic aesthetics and provides a thrilling, stress-inducing ride.

whiplash

The editing is incredibly tight, elevating impromptu drum solos.

Dune

It plays beautifully on the big screen, though newcomers should prepare themselves for an incomplete story and a hefty amount of political build up.
